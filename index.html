You’d be amazed at the reams of excellence that stays below the radar of international markets. Think impeccable local craftsmanship, fresh organic produce made in backyards, or even cutting-edge software made by urban hackers.

In other words: hidden opportunities. The kind usually found where nobody knew or cared to look. Until someone discovers these businesses, potential game-changing, win-win partnerships keep going to waste.

One of such places is our home, Serbia, and the surrounding countries that once made up Yugoslavia (Slovenia, Croatia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Montenegro, Macedonia).

With years of experience and networking, we have these little known transition markets at our fingertips, and we deliver their potential into your hands. We find you the right contact at the right time, and you guys do the rest. And if there are problems, or you need extra advice, we’re always here to help!
